Cavern

A Text-Based Adventure

In this game, the class you choose will affect your combat and fortitude.

Warrior - a sturdy, strapping character who can take a hit but isn't the fastest or most accurate aggressor

Wizard - a brilliant, studious character who can exploit weaknesses but has little defense if the fight is up close and personal

Thief - a deft and quick-witted character who is acquainted with danger but not impervious

You enter a small cavern.

The path on your left leads towards a small hole, just big enough to crawl through.

Will you eat the questionable mushroom?

You take the path on the right, crouching through a low archway.

As you make your way further along, the smell reaches you before your eyes connect with the source.

Its rank blood in your nose, you stand triumphant.

The slot was much more narrow than you anticipated and you pause for a deep breath as your body escapes into the room beyond.

As you enter the gaping maw of the cracked stone, you feel a touch of cool wind caress your cheek.
